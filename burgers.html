Burgers

Description

This is a meat patty between two buns. You can add many different condiments and toppings to make the burger something you would enjoy more.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Cheese
  • lettuce
  • pickles
  • tomato
  • onion
  • ketchup
  • mustard

Steps

  1. For your 1 lb of ground beef into four 1/4 lb round patties
  2. Set you grill to 400 degrees F
  3. As your grill heats prepare your toppings
  4. When you grill reaches temp put your patties on and close the grill for 2-3 minutes
  5. After the 2-3 minutes are up open the grill and flip them and set another 2 minute timer
  6. When the 2 minutes is up add your cheese and let it melt while on the grill for 1 minute
  7. Remove patties from grill and put them on a plate. Turn down the grill heat to 250 degrees F
  8. Toast you buns on the grill with indirect heat (away from the flame)
  9. Put your burgers together and enjoy
