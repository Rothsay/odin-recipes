Tacos

Description

This recipe is all about tacos. I will run you through the basics of making tacos but feel free to add any toppings you would like to make this your own.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • Taco shells (hard or soft)
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Black olives
  • Sour cream
  • Hot sauce

Steps

  1. Brown the ground beef on medium high heat
  2. Drain the excess liquid and add 1/3 cup of water and taco seasoning
  3. Mix together and bring to boil. When it gets to a boil reduce temp to simmer
  4. Stir together every minute or so for four minutes
  5. Put meat on shell of your choice and add toppings of choice
